Accomodation

Given the current COVID guidance we suspect you will want to return home after the ceremony and reception. However there are lots of hotels on Broad Street, near to O'Neill's.
The closest are the Travelodge Central and Novotel, however there are over 10 different hotels within half a mile.
We recommend checking the prices on Google Travel for the best deals.

Transport

Transport will be provided from the Church to the Reception.
We have hired a 72-seat old London Bus to take you the short journey to and from the ceremony at St. George's Church to O'Neill's Broad St.
Please maintain social distancing while travelling on the bus.
Free parking will be available at Church. If you wish to park in Birmingham the nearest car parks are Q-Park Brindleyplace and Euro Car Parks 5 Ways (please check they are open in advance).
If you want to make you own way using public transport, use National Express local bus services 23 or 24 which stop just outside church. The cash fare is £1.50 from town.
Or if using taxi's, we recommend Uber or A2B Radio Cars on 0121 744 1111.
