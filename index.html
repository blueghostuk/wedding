There are lots of hotels on Broad Street, near to O'Neill's.

We recommend checking the prices on Google Travel for the best deals.

Transport

Transport will be provided to, and from, the Church to the Reception.
We have hired a 72-seat old London Bus to take you the short journey to and from the ceremony at St. George's Church to O'Neill's Broad St.
There is limited parking at the Church, so please park in town near to O'Neill's.
Many hotels have their own car parks, but the nearest ones are Q-Park Brindleyplace and Euro Car Parks 5 Ways.
You can catch our bus from nearby O'Neill's/Novotel to Church at 12:30-12:45 - we will confirm the exact time and location nearer to the date due to on-going roadworks.
If you want to make you own way using public transport, use National Express local bus services 23 or 24 which stop just outside church. The cash fare is ¬£1.50 from town.
Or if using taxi's, we recommend Uber or A2B Radio Cars on 0121 744 1111.
