Accomodation

There are lots of hotels on Broad Street, near to O'Neill's.
The closest are the Travelodge Central and Novotel, however there are over 10 different hotels within half a mile.
We recommend checking the prices on Google Travel for the best deals.

Transport

Transport will be provided from the Church to the reception.
We have hired a 72-seat old London Bus to take you the short journey from the ceremony at St. George's to O'Neills.
There is limited parking at the Church, so please park in town near to O'Neills.
Many hotels have their own car parks, but the nearest ones are Q-Park Brindleyplace and Euro Car Parks 5 Ways.
If using public transport, use National Express local bus services 23 or 24 which stop just outside church. The cash fare is £1.50 from town.
If using taxi's, we recommend Uber or A2B Radio Cars on 0121 744 1111.
